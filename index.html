Shikha Goswami is an enthusiastic teacher working in a well-known Global school. It is fulfilling for her to be a teacher since she has always had a dream of becoming one. She coordinated and won the British Council's International School Award for her school. The passion she has for teaching reflects in her teaching style. She is pursuing a postgraduate degree in English literature. Despite her love for all genres, she prefers to read non-fiction. She enjoys reading different genres of writing. She runs her own YouTube channel "Shikha Miss" where she helps teachers and students with a few tricks and tips. You can also connect with her on Instagram shikhamiss_rj. Her story "Momentous report card" reflects her struggle to maintain consistency in her teaching and learning methods. This story, "The momentous report card," is dedicated to all the students of the country who worked hard to continue their studies during the tough times of the pandemic. She tried giving best practices for building the future of students even in the pandemic. She loves her students so much.
